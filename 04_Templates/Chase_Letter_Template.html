A Letter for Chase

This Is Your Time

IKWF State Tournament 2026

#4 Seed

Chase,

I started writing this mid-season, Buddy Boy. The night I first saw it — really saw it — in your eyes. That look. That fire. That thing that separates kids who wrestle from kids who ARE wrestlers.

I went home that night and started typing. I kept rewriting it, week after week, because every time I thought I had it right, you went and did something else that proved me wrong. You kept leveling up. So I stopped trying to write it and started TELLING you instead. Every car ride. Every practice. Every moment between us — I told you.

And you listened. Not with your ears. With your heart.

Now here we are. The night before State. And I'm finishing this letter because you already lived every word I was trying to write.

The Prayer — Your Team

You are never alone on that mat, Chase. You have a team that no one else can see.

Your guardian angels — spiritual warriors assigned by God to protect you, to guide you, to give you strength when yours runs out. They flank you on both sides. They have your back. They are God's warriors, and they do not lose.

And right there with them — your Great-Grandma Paulette. She passed away before you got to know her like we wish you could have, Buddy Boy, but she never left you. She is watching over you right now. She sees you. She knows your heart. She's been praying for this moment since before you were born. When you feel that extra warmth, that calm in the storm — that's her. That's her love wrapping around you like a hug from heaven.

Call upon Jesus. Whisper His name.

"I can do all things through Christ who strengthens me."
— Philippians 4:13

Mom and Dad are right here. Mom was right there with me, bleeding the same way, sacrificing the same way, believing the same way. We are your corner. Always.

All your grandparents helped get you here — Grandma Debbie, Grandpa Bob, Grandma Denise, Grandpa Tommy, Grandma Kim. Every one of them has poured into you.

Your brother Jayden looks up to you more than you know. Your sister Nova — she's your biggest little fan. Uncle Bobby, Uncle Brandon, Aunt Kelsey, Aunt Kassidy, Uncle Tyler — this is a family thing, Buddy Boy. This is what we do. We show up for each other.

The Championship Mindset

"I am the greatest. I said that even before I knew I was."
— Muhammad Ali

Chase, say it. Believe it. Not from arrogance — from preparation. You are the greatest BECAUSE you have outworked everyone. Your preparation is your confidence. Your preparation is your swagger.

"I've failed over and over and over again in my life. And that is why I succeed."
— Michael Jordan

Keep shooting. Keep attacking. Keep believing. Failure is not the opposite of success. It's part of it.

"Everything negative — pressure, challenges — is all an opportunity for me to rise."
— Kobe Bryant

Pressure is a privilege, Buddy Boy. It means you're exactly where you're supposed to be.

"Once you've wrestled, everything else in life is easy."
— Dan Gable

You have already done the hard part. You made it to State. You earned the #4 seed. Now you just go be you.

Rocky vs Drago — You Know Who You Are

Remember Rocky IV? Rocky was the underdog. Drago was the machine — bigger, stronger, "better" on paper. But Rocky had heart. Rocky had something Drago couldn't measure.

Chase, you are Rocky. The #1, #2, #3 seeds — they might look like Drago on paper. But they don't have YOUR heart. They didn't wake up at 5am with you. They didn't run those sprints. They didn't push through when their body said stop.

You did. And that makes you dangerous.

They have the seeding. You have the heart. In wrestling, heart wins.

Lose Yourself

"You better lose yourself in the music, the moment — you own it,
You better never let it go. You only get one shot,
Do not miss your chance to blow.
This opportunity comes once in a lifetime."
— Eminem

This IS that moment. Own it. Be present. Every match. Every period. Every second. Do not leave anything in the tank.

This Is Your Time

You are the #4 seed at IKWF State. Out of every kid in Illinois — YOU are ranked 4th. But seeding is just a number. What matters is who shows up. Who has the heart. Who has been preparing for THIS moment.

That's you.

The Job Ain't Finished

"The job ain't finished."
— Kobe Bryant

You didn't come this far to come this far, Chase. You didn't grind all season just to show up. You did it to WIN.

But here's the truth: you already won. The kid who started this season is not the same kid reading this letter. You grew. You evolved. You became a wrestler. A competitor. A young man who stares pressure in the face and smiles.

So go finish the job. For YOU. Because you deserve to see what happens when preparation meets opportunity.

I am proud of you, Chase. I am proud of WHO you are — kind, loyal, loving, hard-working, faithful.

Those things matter more than any trophy. But tomorrow? Go get the trophy too.

This is your time, Buddy Boy.
I love you.

— Dad
