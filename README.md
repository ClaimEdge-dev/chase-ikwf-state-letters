## About This Collection

This repository contains a complete collection of letters written by a father to his 10-year-old son Chase before the IKWF State Wrestling Tournament. The letters tell the story of a father who started writing mid-season when he first saw "the look" in his son's eyes — that fire that separates kids who wrestle from kids who ARE wrestlers.

The father kept rewriting the letter week after week because Chase kept leveling up. Eventually, he stopped writing and started TELLING Chase instead — every car ride, every practice, every moment between them. Now, the night before State, he's finishing the letter because Chase already lived every word.
